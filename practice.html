Our AI Products

We specialize in designing and developing secure, intelligent AI-powered products that can be customized to meet specific business requirements. Our solutions are built to optimize operational efficiency, support smarter decision-making, and ensure regulatory compliance.

01 .

buoyLIGHT TM

Our AI-powered solution captures container & vehicle data with unmatched speed and accuracy, fully integrated with Terminal Operating Systems (TOS). Deployed at Port Gates, Weighbridges, and Quay Cranes, it boosts efficiency, saves millions, and runs 24/7 even in tough conditions.

02 .

buoySPOT TM

Our AI-powered solution detects and classifies container damages (dents, bends, rust, corrosion, etc.) with precision, ensuring accurate assessments and reducing false claims. It boosts safety, minimizes delays, and helps terminal operators save millions.

03 .

buoySPY TM

Our AI-powered solution ensures 24/7 monitoring of terminals and premises in all conditions. It auto-detects safety violations and alerts instantly to prevent safety breaches. Built with AI, deep learning, and machine learning, it enhances operational safety.

04 .

callMATICS TM

callMatics is an AI-driven call center solution that enables human-like conversations without live agents. It supports unlimited virtual agents, 24/7 operations, and over 100 languages. Automates up to 70% of tasks, saving costs and boosting efficiency.

05 .

planetSCAN TM

PlanetScan combines satellite imagery with GIS to deliver detailed geographic, demographic, and social insights. It helps businesses, planners, and developers identify high-potential areas and optimize strategies with AI-powered analytics.
